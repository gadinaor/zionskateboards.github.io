ABOUT

Zion Skateboards, established in 2004 as a 100% home grown skateboarding company with a goal to promote local skateboarding. Zion Skateboards products are designed by local skaters and emerging urban artists.

We hope we will grow the local skateboarding scene, support local skaters and bring the power back to the skaters wherever we are.

Stay true to your skateboarding,
